About us

The Dance Academy, established since October 2020 aims to provide students with a professional dance curriculum exposing them to well structured training programmes in the various dance disciplines. Students are encouraged to develop their dance skills and performance qualities to the maximum. Students in classical ballet, girls and boys, can enroll as of the age of three years. The school follows the Royal Academy of Dance London Syllabus as well as the Cecchetti Syllabus. These are structured around the physical and motor abilities of the students. At the tender age of three, classes are structured with a 'recreational' focus. Here the importance of 'fun' and 'imagination' are the essential elements of the class. From the age of six, students begin to follow the well structured graded syllabus of the Royal Academy of Dance. Throughout the graded syllabus students are introduced to 'Free Movement' and 'Character.'
